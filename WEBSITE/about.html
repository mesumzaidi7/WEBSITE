About Pakistan

History

Pakistan, officially the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, is a country in South Asia. It was founded by Muhammad Ali Jinnah on August 14, 1947. The capital of Pakistan is Islamabad. 1971, a crisis in Pakistan resulted in a war between India, Pakistan, and the seperation of East Pakistan, which is now modern day Bangladesh.

Food & Drink

Pakistan is known for the amount of spices they put in their dishes. The most popular dish is biryani, which is a rice dish that can go with many types of meats. It is my personal favourite because of the amazing flavour. When you are in Pakistan, you will see many street food, which tastes really amazing.A famous drink is lassi, which is a yogurt drink best served cold. Personally, I am not a fan of it because I do not like the taste of yogurt.

Sports

Pakistan is known for playing two sports, cricket and field hockey. The national sport is field hockey, but cricket is the most popular. The cricket team is good team, led by captain Babar Azam, but the team was better in the 90s. which is when they won the ICC World Cup in 1992. Pakistan also has a women's cricket team.
